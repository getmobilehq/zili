— How it works

Upload. Present. Share.

Three steps from raw HTML to a shareable presentation. No editor, no template, no porting. The artifact you uploaded is the artifact your audience sees.

Step 01

Upload your HTML

Drag a file, paste raw HTML, or point to a public URL. Single-file artifacts work out of the box. Multi-file uploads with assets ship in V1.5.

Step 02

We render it faithfully

Your artifact runs in a sandboxed iframe with full CSS fidelity. We detect whether it's a slide deck or a document, and present accordingly. One-click override if we guess wrong.

Step 03

Share or present

Get a public link with a beautiful Open Graph preview. Present in fullscreen with keyboard navigation. Embed it anywhere. Send it to your client, your team, or your audience.

— FAQ

Questions, answered.

Why not just use PowerPoint or Google Slides?
They were designed for object-based slides — text frames, shapes, images. LLMs generate HTML and CSS. The import path is lossy by design: gradients vanish, fonts swap, layouts break. Zili renders the HTML directly, so what your LLM produced is what your audience sees.
Which LLMs work with Zili?
Any tool that outputs HTML — Claude, ChatGPT, v0, Lovable, Bolt, Cursor, hand-written, anything. If it's valid HTML in a single file (or a zip with assets, in V1.5), Zili will render it.
Is it secure to upload my HTML to your servers?
Yes. Every artifact renders in a sandboxed iframe with strict Content Security Policy and no parent-frame access. Your scripts can't see anything outside their sandbox. We scan uploads for known-malicious patterns before render.
Can I edit artifacts in Zili?
Not in V1. Zili is a renderer and presenter, not an editor. We're shipping "regenerate with a prompt" in V2.5 — describe a change, the LLM rewrites the relevant section, Zili re-renders. For now: iterate in your LLM of choice, re-upload to Zili. Your share URL stays the same.
What happens after the 3-month free promo?
You keep using Zili. Free tier stays free (with a small watermark on shared decks and a 25-artifact cap). Pro is $12/month. If you signed up during the promo and upgrade within 30 days of its end, you lock in 50% off Pro for life — $6/month, forever.
Can my audience see the deck without an account?
Yes. Public share links work for anyone — no signup, no friction. They get a presenter UI with keyboard nav, fullscreen, and mobile swipe gestures.
What about teams?
V1 is individual-only. Team workspaces with shared libraries and collaboration arrive in V2 (around 3 months post-launch). If you need it sooner, let us know — we're prioritising based on demand.
